Know About us

Restore Ability Services

Restore Ability Services is a service provider that was established in 2023 to meet the needs of clients based on a person centered approach where we put the client at the center of everything. We provide a wide variety of services to all people of all ages. Restore was established by the director who has worked as a support worker dealing with different clients for more than ten years. The director also worked as an accountant for other service providers for more than five years.As a result of the passion of the director, Restore Ability was founded. The client is at the center of everything, we are just here to support their vision and to help them live the best life they can. The client is the driver and we are just assisting.

More About Us

Restore Ability Services is dedicated to a person-centered approach, putting clients at the heart of everything we do. Our team supports your vision and helps you live your best life, with you in control every step of the way.

We provide services to people of all ages and abilities. Whether you need assistance with daily living, personal care, community engagement, or other support, we are here to help meet your needs.

We offer a broad range of services, including domestic assistance, personal care, travel and transport, companionship, community engagement, and meal preparation, among others, tailored to your individual needs.

Getting started is easy. Contact us to discuss your needs, and we’ll work with you to create a tailored plan that supports your goals. From the first conversation, our focus is on your vision and how we can help you achieve it.

Our team works closely with clients to understand their unique goals and needs. We provide tailored support to help clients achieve their aspirations, enhance their independence, and increase their participation in the community.

Restore Ability Services was founded by a director with over ten years of experience as a support worker and five years as an accountant for other service providers. Driven by passion and experience, the director established Restore Ability to empower clients to take control of their lives.
